Set up school emails 🏫

Connect your child's school and get all their letters, events and newsletters in one place.

Gmail required. Miru reads school emails directly from your Gmail inbox using Google's secure login. After you sign up you'll be taken to Google to grant access — this takes about 30 seconds.
If your school emails go to Outlook, Yahoo or another provider, the auto-read feature won't work. WhatsApp commands will still work, but emails won't be fetched automatically.
⚠️ Google warning — what to expect: When connecting Gmail you may see a Google screen saying "This app isn't verified". This is because Miru is currently in testing mode. It's safe to proceed — click "Advanced" then "Go to Miru (unsafe)" to continue. We only read school emails and never modify or share your inbox.
